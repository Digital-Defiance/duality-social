About

One of the weird political things that emerged since 2016 is the idea of everything needing to be presented with "both sides" of any issue.

When OpenAI became available to us, the creator had the idea that it might be interesting to have a social network where every post is presented with a roughly opposite view and tone. So, if you post "I love bacon", the "other side" might be "I hate bacon".

This social network takes every post you make and presents it with the AI's idea of the Devil's Advocate view. Users then vote on which they think is the human's view (and logicallly the other is the AI's view).

There is also a separate reaction (like, love, hug, sad, etc) you can do on only one of the two views of the post.

This "social network" is an experiment, and an expensive one at that.

OpenAI is relatively expensive, in addition to other database, hosting, bandwidth, and other overhead necessary for Duality Social to function.

Duality Social is being presented as a Pay to Peek social experiment. We will create invitation codes with a limited number of uses, and you will be able to share those codes with your friends.

In order for this site to continue to exist, these things need to happen:

  • People need to use the site and enjoy it.
  • People need to share the site with their friends.
  • People need to pay enough for the site to at least break even.
  • Members need to remember to respect one another, and to follow the terms and rules we are bound to through our collective use of OpenAI.
  • Members need to report bad behavior immediately. Reports on a post or user will be reviewed by the site administrators and appropriate action will be taken as soon as possible.
  • * Reports on posts due to the AI's response may take down the post, but will not affect the user's account standing.
  • If the Peek funds are sufficient, additional moderation staff may be hired to help with the review process.
  • Members have a duty to report anything in breach of OpenAI's terms which will risk our ability to operate.
  • Be good, enjoy the mystery.
